# CHANAKYA SSC — Official Trailer Script
## "The Warrior's Code" — 90 Seconds

**Tone:** Cinematic. Serious. Human. Not fake motivational.
**Music:** Starts silent → low tabla pulse → builds to orchestral surge → final note of sitar.
**Language:** Hindi V.O. with English subtitles. Text cards in English.
**Format:** YouTube / Instagram Reels (16:9 or 9:16 crop)

---

## SCENE 1 — THE WEIGHT (0:00 – 0:18)

**Visual:** Black screen.
A single flame of a candle. 2 AM.
Slow push-in on a young man at a table covered in books.
His face: exhausted. Not defeated. Still fighting.

**Sound:** Clock ticking. Pages turning. Faint sound of a sleeping household.

**V.O. (whispered Hindi):**
> *"Kitne saal ho gaye?"*
> *(How many years has it been?)*

**Text on screen — appears word by word:**
> FEES. TRAVEL. COACHING. WAITING.

**V.O.:**
> *"Ghar walon ki aankhon mein sawal hai. Tum khamosh rahte ho."*
> *(There are questions in your family's eyes. You stay silent.)*

> *"Ek aur form. Ek aur attempt. Ek aur baar — 'is baar ho jayega'."*
> *(One more form. One more attempt. One more — 'this time it'll happen'.)*

---

## SCENE 2 — THE BRUTAL TRUTH (0:18 – 0:34)

**Visual:** Black screen.
White text appears, slowly, like a verdict being handed down.

**V.O. (measured, direct):**
> *"Tum mehnat nahi karte — yeh jhooth hai."*
> *(You don't work hard — that's a lie.)*

> *"Tum kamzor ho — yeh bhi jhooth hai."*
> *(You're weak — that's a lie too.)*

Pause.

**Full screen text — GOLD, large:**
> YOU DON'T HAVE A SYSTEM.

**V.O.:**
> *"Sirf ek cheez kami hai."*
> *(Only one thing is missing.)*

---

## SCENE 3 — THE CHANAKYA REVEAL (0:34 – 0:52)

**Visual:**
Ancient Sanskrit text dissolving into modern code.
A lotus blooming in reverse — becoming a strategic diagram.
Then — the CHANAKYA SSC wordmark burns into frame, gold on black.

**Music:** Sitar note. Deep. Resonant. Then the orchestral bed begins.

**V.O. (deeper, with weight):**
> *"Chanakya ne kabhi nahi kaha: 'Aur mehnat karo'."*
> *(Chanakya never said: 'Work harder'.)*

> *"Unhone kaha: 'Pehle apni kami samjho. Phir vaar karo'."*
> *(He said: 'First understand your weakness. Then strike'.)*

**Text appears:**
> KAUTILYA DIDN'T WIN BATTLES.
> HE MADE THEM UNNECESSARY.

---

## SCENE 4 — THE SYSTEM (0:52 – 1:18)

**Visual:** Rapid, clean cuts. Each 1.5 seconds.

- Aspirant opens CHANAKYA SSC. Mock test begins.
- Timer counting down. Questions flashing.
- Test submits. SCORE: 124/200.
- Diagnosis appears: *"Score Leak: Concept Gap in Polity — 4 questions lost."*
- Smart Note opens — Article 21. Not a textbook page.
  A story. Bold. Visual. Alive.
- Mnemonic card: *"21 = Life first. Liberty next. Dignity always."*
- Mind map expands on screen.
- 7-day plan generates.
- Aspirant revising. Confident. Different.

**V.O. (building in pace):**
> *"Har galat jawab ek diagnosis hai."*
> *(Every wrong answer is a diagnosis.)*

> *"Kahan leak ho raha hai score — CHANAKYA bata deta hai."*
> *(Where your score is leaking — CHANAKYA tells you.)*

> *"Har topic ek kahani ban jaata hai — bhulna mushkil ho jaata hai."*
> *(Every topic becomes a story — impossible to forget.)*

> *"Phir ek plan. Ek path. Gate by Gate."*
> *(Then a plan. A path. Gate by Gate.)*

**Music peaks.**

---

## SCENE 5 — THE WARRIOR WALKS IN (1:18 – 1:28)

**Visual:**
The same aspirant from Scene 1 — but different.
Clean. Calm. Walking into an examination hall.
He sits. Opens the paper. A slight nod.

**V.O. (quiet, certain):**
> *"Aaj woh tayaar hai."*
> *(Today, he is ready.)*

> *"Is baar nahi — system se."*
> *(Not by chance — by system.)*

---

## SCENE 6 — THE RESULT (1:28 – 1:32)

**Visual:** Black screen.

A single sound: the click of a keyboard.

Text appears — small at first, then filling the screen:

> **SELECTED**

Gold glow. Fade.

---

## END CARD (1:32 – 1:40)

**Visual:** Clean black. CHANAKYA SSC logo in gold center.

**Tagline appears, word by word:**
> YOUR STRATEGY.
> YOUR SELECTION.

**Small text below:**
> chanakyassc.in

**Music:** Final sitar note. Fades to silence.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**What to brief your video editor:**
- Color grade: Near-black base. Gold highlights only.
- No stock footage of "happy students." Real aspirant energy only.
- Slow motion for emotional beats, fast cuts for the system sequence.
- Text animations: slow fade-in, not aggressive kinetic typography.
- End card should feel like a stamp — not a sales pitch.

**Music reference:**
- Hans Zimmer — Time (Inception)
- A.R. Rahman — Maa Tujhe Salaam (orchestral base)
- Add tabla + sitar on top for Indian identity.

**Possible actors:**
- Real SSC aspirants. Not models. Authenticity is the brand.
